About me

A young enthusiastic student excited to kick start a career. Armed with great teamwork and problem-solving skills. I hope to combine these with my love for technology with the ultimate goal of innovating the industry I am about to enter. I thrive when faced with a challenge and actively seek to broaden my horizons with new tasks or mastering my current specialisations.

Industry knowledge

MEng student at Royal Holloway, University of London studying Electronic Engineering. Interested in digital signal processing, biomedical engineering, renewable energy systems, advanced communication systems, embedded systems and control engineering.

Able to work solo or part of a team with both agile and scrum methods for project development. Track record of conceptualize, research, design, develop and prototype of electronics. Knowledge of industry standard software including: Office 365, NI Multisim, NI LabVIEW, NI Ultiboard, Adobe CC, MATLAB and SIMULINK. High quality production of professional documentation such as SRS and lab reports.

Several years of experience in the IT industry with membership of the IET, currently progressing towards professional CEng registration. Always on the lookout for ambitious projects led by dedicated teams for both summer internships and a year in industry placement.
